# AlGaN/GaN-Based Transistor Development
### Ohmic Contact and Schottky Gate Contact Characterization

BSc thesis project — Budapest University of Technology and Economics (BME),
Department of Electronic Devices. Supervisor: Dr. Péter Lajos Neumann.

## Overview
Electrical characterization of the ohmic and Schottky gate contacts of
AlGaN/GaN HEMTs, fabricated in-house on a commercial AlGaN/GaN epitaxial
wafer (NTT-AT). The work focuses on extracting key contact parameters from
forward I–V measurements and assessing the influence of gate metal choice
(Au vs. Pt) on contact quality.

## Methods
- **Ohmic contacts:** Transmission Line Model (TLM) to extract contact
  resistance, specific contact resistivity, and sheet resistance.
- **Schottky gate contacts:** forward I–V characterization, with parameter
  extraction via the thermionic emission model and Cheung's method
  (ideality factor, Schottky barrier height, series resistance).
- **Instruments:** probe station with Keithley 2450 SMU; analysis in MATLAB.
